About Pithia

Pithia is focused on enabling the end-to-end growth of the blockchain, focusing on the ecosystem around RChain's performant blockchain.

We grow business and meaningful relationships in three directions:

  1. Upstream to Limited Partners, investment banks, and analysts;
  2. Downstream to entrepreneurs and their companies and projects; and
  3. Laterally to industry partners and our Blockchain Standards Working Groups (initially, Identity, Storage, and Governance)

Pithia provides access to capital, education, and hands-on business, regulatory, and technical expertise, as well as market intelligence and blockchain governance.

We also provide developer education programs to enable developers to architect solutions that take advantage of RChain.
