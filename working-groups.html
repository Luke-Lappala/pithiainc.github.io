Working Groups

Standards for consistency and interoperability are keys to long-term growth and wide adoption. Pithia and industry-leading product companies are creating standards through working groups in several key areas, starting with identity and storage.

Identity Working Group

The Identity working group is launching now with five organizations: LifeID, NuID, TrustedKey, Sovrin, and Verif-y, with one more organization expected to join soon. More information on this working group can be found on the Identity Working Group Page.

Upcoming Working Groups

Details of the Storage working group will be announced in the coming months.

Additional working groups for transactions, records, governance ,and consumer and social interaction and activity will be announced as well.
