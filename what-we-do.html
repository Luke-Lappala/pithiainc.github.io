WHAT WE DO

We Make Portfolio Investments

In both horizontal and vertical markets, we make equity investments in startup companies, including early stage investments.

We Support Our Investors

Our shared services, including finance, accounting, market and company analysis and due diligence, program management, and legal support, are available to our limited and corporate partners as well as to our investments.

We Help You Refactor Your Existing Effort

Whether you have a current blockchain effort or business process, we provide technical and financial support.

We Sponsor Promotion and Education

Across all addressable markets, we sponsor those educating and evangelizing the value of the RChain platform as well as our partner and portfolio companies.

We Build Partnerships

We work with organizations both existing and new providing parts of the whole product solution, enabling faster time to market for the RChain ecosystem.
